Filming Stages of Cancer

This tool is used to clarify the stages of cancer based on the patient's information such as age and tumor size. It follows a simplified algorithm and is not a substitute for professional medical advice. Please consult your doctor for an accurate assessment.

There is a relationship between tumor size, patient age and cancer stage. The smaller the tumor, and the younger the patient, the less serious the stage of the disease. Very small tumours and young patients are often in the first phase of the disease. While large-sized tumors and elderly patients are usually at advanced stages. These factors are used to determine the progress of cancer and choose the most appropriate treatment plan
